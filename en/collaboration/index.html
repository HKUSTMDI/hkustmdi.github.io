Virtual Doctor for GIST

This project targets end-to-end care for gastrointestinal stromal tumors (GIST), building a "virtual doctor" that serves both clinicians and patients. Powered by a multimodal foundation model, the system integrates CT, endoscopy, ultrasound, pathology whole-slide imaging (WSI), clinical report text, and follow-up data collected via the mini-program. On this basis it executes a closed loop of risk stratification → individualized treatment planning → therapy efficacy and recurrence-risk prediction → follow-up and medication management. For clinicians, it provides an MDT (multidisciplinary team) decision dashboard, standardized documentation, and a traceable evidence chain; for patients, it offers medication reminders, symptom reporting, and risk alerts. Technically, the system combines our self-developed multimodal encoder and diagnostic-therapeutic agent, aiming to significantly reduce information curation and decision-making costs in real clinical workflows while improving consistency and interpretability of care.
